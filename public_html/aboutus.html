About Us

Unfade company is a video games company that was founded by Poh Choo Meng, William Choong, Choo Zhi Yan and Chew Wei Chung in May 2019. Unfade company located at Bayan Baru, Pulau Pinang of Malaysia. It is a website that develops a lot of games that are being free to play or paid to play. Game types that developed by Unfade were based on horror games, role-playing games and first person shooter games. Those video games operated by using C++ and Java. Games that are developed allow players to multiplayer, sell their special ingame items in the marketplace, top-up by using virtual coins (U coins) to gain special perk in the games. Currently, the games that are under Unfade are “ParanormalHK”, “Final Fantasy XIII”. With just released for 2 months, the company has reached 10k users for playing the game that was made by Unfade.
